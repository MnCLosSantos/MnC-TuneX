TuneX R69 Interface

Welcome to the TuneX R69 Interface here you can manage applying tunes via your TuneX OBD PlugIN, decrypting files via your TuneX Tunes Database and removing your TuneX devices. Make sure to remove your devices as they may be seized.

Decryption Settings

Files must be decrypted to tune a vehicle, Encrypted files are not recognised by the ecu or plug in you must decrypt them before trying to tune your vehicle, do that here. Be aware it is illegal to tune your vehicles ecu without declaring to your insurance provider.

AutoDrive Settings

Welcome to AutoDrive once you have set a waypoint you can use these. Why drive when you don't have to, times are changing and so are vehicles.

Driftmode Settings

Driftmode enables controlled oversteer. Use responsibly on closed roads or race tracks. Illegal use of drift kits may lead to vehicle seizures or legal consequences.

Vehicle Assist Settings

Welcome to Vehicle Assist Settings, here you can turn off and on your Vehicle Assists unlocking the full potential of your vehicle. Caution is advised when traveling with no assists and your insurance may not cover you.
